Support/FAQs

Payment and billing information

The amount charged does not match my subscription

If you have been charged an amount that does not match your subscription, please send us details to the email address and we will inform you of the estimated time for your refund: culers.support@fcbarcelona.cat

I have been charged twice

If you have been charged twice, please send us details to the email address and we will inform you of the estimated time for your refund: culers.support@fcbarcelona.cat

I’ve changed my mind. Can I exercise my right to return?

According to the contract policy, the subscription does not have the right to return. Should you wish to cancel your subscription, you should access the “My Account” section and cancel. Cancelation will be effective at the end of the current billing period.

How can I receive an invoice for my subscription?

You will be able to receive the invoice related to your subscription by contacting our customer support service, providing us with the necessary details: culers.support@fcbarcelona.cat

How can I apply a discount code?

You can apply your discount code during the payment process, introducing it in the specific section. The discount amount will be subtracted from the total.
